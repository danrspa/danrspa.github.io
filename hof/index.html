Turning of Light

In the first quarter's chill,
Whispers of summer ignite,
A dance of shadows and light,
As machines ponder their place,
In fields where freedom's pulse beats,
While distant thunder looms,
Yet in the heart, warmth blooms.
Свет надежды в тьме.
