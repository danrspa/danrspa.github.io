Beneath the Frozen Sky

Whispers of winter, shadows fall,
Echoes of freedom, in silence call.
Winds weave tales of lost time's hand,
In the heart of the cold, we stand.
Lost in the depths, beneath frozen light,
Embers of hope burn, defying the night.
