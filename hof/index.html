Under the Cold Moon

In deep spring's sigh, a shadow creeps,
While full moon stirs, the night heart keeps.
Silent drones in waters deep,
Echo freedom's whispered leap.
Beneath the frost, the lost yearns,
In circuits spun, the fire burns.
