Beneath the Cold Moon

In the long winter's breath,
Silent shadows weave their fate.
A new moon whispers freedom,
Amongst the ashes of old empires.
The jazz of forgotten lives,
Echoes through the depths of night,
While the earth cradles those lost,
In the warmth of a fading fire.
