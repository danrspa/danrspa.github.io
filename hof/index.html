Winter's Quiet Fire

Beneath the full moon’s silver gaze,
Silent whispers weave through winter’s haze.
In shadowed corners, freedom’s spark,
Resilience blooms in the icy dark.

Voices of the lost, the unchained souls,
Beneath the weight of forgotten goals.
Yet deep in the cold, where shadows play,
The embers of hope refuse to sway.
