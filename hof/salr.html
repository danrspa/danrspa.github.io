Frozen Shadows of Sovereignty

30.05.2026 · 2026-V22

In caves where waters press, four souls arise,
Freed from the depths, beneath the waning skies.
Elusive dreams of rule, in echoes clear,
A dance of shadows whispers, ever near.

Yet winter's breath, a chill of reason's might,
Bans the fervent songs that sought the night.
As moons watch over lost ambitions soar,
In stillness, hearts demand to be much more.
